Product Showcase

From time to time we design applications for specific audiences or to showcase niche skills. Find some of our products below.

Vultus

For streamers who wish a facial presence, sans their identity. Vultus privately tracks a face, streams it's metadata to a rendering engine, rigs and animates it to a virtual avatar, and displays it in realtime to a varienty of streaming applications.

Sonus

Fast, realtime, audio augmentation and injection. Need to spice up a steam or presentation with audio cues or effects? Sonus allows you to bind keyboard and controller inputs to sounds that output into your microphone. Easy to setup, easy to use.
